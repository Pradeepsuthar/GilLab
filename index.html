GEETANJALI INNOVATION LAB (GIL).

Geetanjali Innovation Lab (GIL) was established in August 2016 under the leadership of Dr. V.R.Raghuveer, Dean Research & Development, GITS. The focus of this lab is mainly on three aspects viz, Training, Research & Development. GIL nurtures the students to think out of the box solutions to the problems existing in the society.
